The Resonance

A descent into the resonant frequencies of the subconscious. 'Shadow Work' serves as a sonic exploration of the textures hidden beneath the surface of silence. Produced by Aris Thorne in a subterranean studio, the track leverages modular synthesis to create breathing, organic soundscapes that refuse to sit still.

Recorded: Berlin, Winter 2023
Mastered: Obsidian Audio Lab

Technical Architecture

The rhythmic foundation is built upon granulated field recordings of crumbling architectural structures. Every transient is meticulously shaped to evoke the sensation of tactile pressure.
